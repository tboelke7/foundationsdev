For fifteen years, the game was simple: rank on Google, get found. That's still
true — but a second front just opened up, and most contractors have no idea it
exists.

More and more homeowners are skipping the Google search entirely and asking
ChatGPT or Gemini: *"Who's a good kitchen remodeler near Brandon?"* And the AI
answers with a short list of names. If you're not on that list, you don't exist
to that homeowner.

## Why this matters now

The contractors who show up in AI answers aren't there by luck. They're there
because their web presence gives the AI something solid to point to: a real
website, an active Google Business Profile, consistent reviews, and content that
clearly says who they are and where they work.

That's the same foundation that's always won local SEO. The AI shift didn't
change the fundamentals — it just raised the stakes for getting them right.

## What to do about it

Start with the basics, done well: a fast site you own, a fully optimized Google
Business Profile, and a steady stream of real reviews. Those three things are
what make you findable — whether the homeowner is typing into Google or asking
an AI.
